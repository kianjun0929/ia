Mission, Vision, and Objectives

The APU Badminton Club recognizes by a combined commitment and collaboration with the club officers, members, badminton enthusiasts and other organizations in the community.

The main objective and concept of this club is to help APU students to become physically and mentally fit and also to train all beginners and those interested to play badminton. Below is the declaration of our Mission and Vision statements, respectively.

Vision Mission
The vision of APU Badminton Club is to be a good badminton player as we enjoy the friendship of one another, to promote the idea of sportsmanship, healthy lifestyle, the development of skills, the well-being derived from physical activity, and the social aspects which the APU Badminton Club has to offer. Our mission is to help badminton players improve their skills and to foster a constructive training environment for players of all levels. Though we welcome players of all levels, we expect a growth-oriented mindset from all players in the club.
Description

  • The purpose of the club is to bring students together who are interested in playing badminton, to teach and learn from one another, to have fun, to get a good workout, and to make social connections

  • Our purpose is to promote the sport of badminton for competitive and recreational purposes within the campus.
Badminton Court

Badminton facilities are made available at the Pioneer Badminton Centre which is located at Endah Parade. Arrangements have been made for courts to be made available exclusively for the students of the University at specific times during the week. Coaching sessions are also held to provide training to students, including to those who are part of the University’s Badminton Team.
Specific Objectives:
  1. To assemble people with the common interest of participating in and/or promoting recreational and competitive badminton.
  2. To enrich and enhance the skills in playing badminton.
  3. To offer coaching and competitive opportunities in badminton.
  4. To maintain affiliation to competitive local competition for the purpose of providing its members with the opportunity to play representative badminton for the Club.
  5. To ensure that all members of the Club are treated equitably with care and respect.
